Introduction

In optimization theory and machine learning filed, vector/matrix value functions with vector/matrix are commonly seen and the derivative of such functions are often required. When calculating derivatives with respect to vectors and matrices, there often comes the problem of transpose. In many books there are different ways of dealing this problem. In this article we provide an elegant, convenient way for dealing such problem.
